OUR SERVICES

Based in the industrial hub of Hosur, Tamil Nadu, India, NCS Industries offers cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality. We are known for producing complex and highly precise engineering components that are challenging to manufacture, meeting the needs of demanding applications across automotive, Medical industry, industrial Equipment, Aerospace industry and defense sectors.

Our core specialization lies in CNC machining, centreless grinding services, and the production of small to medium-sized mechanical parts and assemblies—where precision, consistency, and turnaround time matter the most.

Precision Engineering, Built on Innovation

Founded in 2021, NCS Industry is an ISO 9001:2015 certified engineering company based in Hosur, Tamil Nadu. We specialize in precision machining, combining advanced CNC technology with skilled engineering expertise to deliver world-class components.

Over the years, we’ve built a reputation for precision, quality, and reliability—manufacturing complex parts that demand extreme accuracy and consistency. Our capabilities include CNC Turning and Centerless Grinding for small to medium-sized mechanical components.

We take pride in achieving accuracy up to ±0.003 mm and ensuring every product meets strict quality standards. Competitive in price and uncompromising in quality, NCS Industry stands for trust, precision, and continuous improvement.
